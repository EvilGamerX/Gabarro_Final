Questions that need answering.

Here are some commonly asked questions. Feel free to contact us if there are any additional concerns.

Students:

Why choose our online mentoring services?

By using Knearn's platform, you can ask any question to mentors from different universities and companies to learn more about student life and work life. On top of that, you will stay anonymous as a student user, so there's no need for apprehension. Furthermore, it's absolutely free to you.

What information do mentors receive about me?

Your information will be kept strictly confidential. Your student account will be completely anonymous, with the mentor knowing only the subject you wish to learn more about and your user-name. In each question, you can provide any information necessary for your mentor to best help you as you see fit.

How long do responses take?

Allot a specific amount of days (3-10 days) in which you would like your question to be answered by mentor(s). If the mentors miss your questions after the specified date, simply click REPOST to send the question back on top of the queue.

How do I change my password?

Click the “Account” dropdown at the top right of the website, and change your password from the “Change Password” tab, or click here.

Mentors:

Why become a mentor for Knearn?

We all wish we could go back in time and give our younger selves some advice. Unfortunately, we still don’t have time machines. But, there is no reason for this advice to go to waste. Pass on your knowledge to the current generation of students.

This knowledge can take many forms:

  • Life experience
  • Career & internship advice
  • Extracurriculars and hobbies
  • Choosing a school and major
  • Student organization support
  • Best places to eat in around campus

How do I become a mentor?

Take a minute and sign up on our Create an Account page. After, head over to the Profile Tab and fill in the appropriate information, including documents such as your resume, transcripts, and links to other social network profiles; the more information you provide, the more categories you will be able to answer.

Once all the information is included, click “Request Approval” on the bottom of the page. We will then be able to review your information. Upon completion, you will receive an email with the approved topics. At this point, you can return to your profile page and check the categories you wish to be a mentor in for confirmation. (Note: you will only be able to check the boxes you received approval for in the email).

Update your profile, and start spreading your knowledge!

What topics can I mentor in?

As of now, we broke mentoring into two major categories—School Questions and Workplace Questions. School Questions would involve topics pertaining to finding the right college, graduate school, major(s), minors and more. Workplace Questions pertain to anything career-related, such as finding the right internships or full time jobs.

Why do I need a profile?

Profiles give you the ability to share your accomplishments and to provide specifics on what you’ve done before, during, and after college. More detailed profiles would allow students to gain a better understanding of your background and thus give them a better sense of how you could guide them. Since we provide a search function for keywords, be sure to include a detailed explanation of your successes.

How do I answer questions?

Under the “Questions” tab, you will be able to see if anyone has submitted a question that you are qualified to answer. You can view your active discussions under the Public, Private, and Group tabs, and those you have completed in the “Closed Questions” tab.

Are there time obligations?

While mentors are not on the clock, questions are usually posted on the platform for up to 10 days before it gets closed.

What if a student tries to contact me outside of the site?

We urge all conversations between mentors and students to be done within the constraints of the site to ensure efficiency, privacy, and safety.

How do I change my password?

Click the “Account” dropdown at the top right of the website, click “Edit Settings / Profiles” and click the “Change Password” sidebar tab, or click here.

How do I link my student and mentor accounts?

After you have created both a student account and a mentor account, log into your mentor account. Click the “Account” drop down, click “Edit Settings / Profiles” and click the “Link Accounts” sidebar tab. There you will need to provide your student account’s username, password, and your mentor account password.

Groups:

What is a group?

A group allows users and mentors to join smaller “communities” (up to 10 people) where anyone can create and thread and reply to each other’s posts. Think of it as a “study group” that you can create with your colleagues.

How do I create or leave a group?

To create a group, go to the “Questions” tab and click “My Groups”. Select “Join/Leave Groups” and type in your desired group name in the “Create Group” field. The group will be created and you will be provided with the passcode to distribute to members (student or mentor) you wish to join.

Similarly, to leave a group, go to the “Questions” tab and click “My Groups”. Select “Join/Leave Groups” and click the yellow “Leave Group” button next to the group you wish to leave.

How do I join a group?

After getting the passcode from the group creator, go to the “Questions” tab and click “My Groups.” Select “Join/Leave Groups” and enter the passcode into the "Join Group" field. And now, you are in!

What can I do in a group?

Just like a study group you hold with your friends, Knearn members who have joined a group can interact with each other via our posting/thread system. Any member can create a new thread, view current threads, post in active threads, and freely interact with fellow members.

Why can’t I join a group?

You may have entered in the wrong passcode or the group is currently at maximum capacity (10 members). Check with the group creator to be certain.
